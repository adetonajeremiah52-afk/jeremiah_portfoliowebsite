About Us

AMBA GUARDS LIMITED is a renowed private security providing company that was incoporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act, 2020 with registration No. 1791459, as a commercial security outfit in Nigeria by the Corporate Affairs Commission, to carry on the business of a security firm and provide for the physical protection of lives and property.
We are committed to providing the best security services to our customers. We are private security company with resourceful experience. We provide trained unarmed security operatives with expert capabilities to handle any situation. Our team is highly professional and proactive, which allows our clients to trust and depend on us completely
